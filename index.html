Intro

My name is Daniel Richmond, a long-time Healthare Administrator who, at 39 years of age, decided to delve into software engineering!

I have lived in many placecs. Born in Anchorage, Alaska, I moved to Fresno, California in 1986. It was in 1998- my high school graduation year- I decided to move to Franklin, Tennessee. Though my previous consulting career had me relocate to a number of other states, I always end up back in Tennessee.

About Me

Well into my term working in the healthcare industry, I held a Project Manager role for the design and development of a telemedicine platform and EHR for the eldercare industry.

It was the first of its kind... and I was a part of it!

I partnered with the IT profesional who directed the technical developments and quickly found myself fascinated by what he was able to create with some words on a screen. That's what started my interest in software engineering, though it has grown, immensely, since. There is something to say about being able to see the results of something you create- visually!

I've always been a people person, ever since a child. I was "the" social butterfly.

Working in team settings came naturally. This natural ability has fostered my career's growth and led to many valuable friendships.
